TuneTalker

  • Developed from October 2024 to May 2025 for capstone class
  • TuneTalker is an all-in-one music discussion, cataloging, statistics, and recommendation application for those who love music. TuneTalker uses the Spotify API to analyze your listening history and build a personalized profile. This profile enables users to:
    • Rate and discuss music
    • Share posts and ratings
    • Track listening habits
    • Discover new music tailored to their tastes
